Company timeline

2017
Med & Skin founded in Brussels. Launch of injectable product distribution.
2018
Exclusive Benelux distributor for Apriline, Medic Control Peel, Mesaltera, Skinasil and DSD de Luxe.
2019
Relocated to new office with dedicated training rooms. Exclusive distribution of WiQo Med / PRX‑T33® for Belgium & Luxembourg.
2020
Opened branches in Paris and London. Exclusive distributor of Holy Land for Belgium, Luxembourg, France and the UK.
2021–2025
Launched online store, expanded Skin Tech distribution, broadened portfolio, training centres and international congress participation.
